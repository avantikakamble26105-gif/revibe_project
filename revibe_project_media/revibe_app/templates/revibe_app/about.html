About Revibe

Revibe is a therapy-focused music platform that helps you discover and create playlists based on your current mood. Whether you want to relax, focus, meditate, or just find calm in the background, Revibe curates sounds that fit your state of mind.

Built with Django, Revibe is designed to be simple, accessible, and supportive of mental wellness through the power of music. It’s more than just playlists — it’s about creating the right vibe for you.
